      8 reasons why picking up surfing is a swell idea

      When people think of surfers, they think, duuuude, toned body, wavy hair and incredible balance. What the average Joe Soap doesn't realise, is that the benefits surfing can contribute to a persons life are monumental. Hear me out...
      1. A great reason to wake up early, as morning is the best time to surf.
      2. A salty bath to improve the health of your skin.
      3. Disconnecting from the virtual world and reconnecting with the present boostsing ones overall mood.
      4. Excellent rewards for your immune system, cardiovascular system, your muscles, timing and balance.
      5. Helps improve your ability to stay calm in situations where you are not in control. (Getting smashed by waves lol)
      6. Meeting new like minded friends or significant other.
      7. Catching epic waves and having fun!
      8. Every beach has dogs, fish + chips and ice-cream... need I say more?
